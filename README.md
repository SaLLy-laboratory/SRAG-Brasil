# Bayesian spatio-temporal modeling of severe acute respiratory syndrome in Brazil: A comparative analysis across pre-, during, and post-COVID-19 eras

Directory created to share the main files and the codes used in the construction of the data tables of the article "Bayesian spatio-temporal modeling of severe acute respiratory syndrome in Brazil: A comparative analysis across pre-, during, and post-COVID-19 eras" of the journal "Infectious Disease Modelling".

Abstract - This paper presents an investigation into the spatio-temporal dynamics of Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS) across the diverse health regions of Brazil from 2016 to 2024. Leveraging extensive datasets that include SARS cases, climate data, hospitalization records, and COVID-19 vaccination information, our study employs a Bayesian spatio temporal generalized linear model to capture the intricate dependencies inherent in the dataset. The analysis reveals significant variations in the incidence of SARS cases over time, particularly during and between the distinct eras of pre-COVID-19, during, and post COVID-19. Our modeling approach accommodates explanatory variables such as humidity, temperature, and COVID-19 vaccine doses, providing a comprehensive understanding of the factors influencing SARS dynamics. Our modeling revealed unique temporal trends in SARS cases for each region, resembling neighborhood patterns. Low temperature and high humidity were linked to decreased cases, while in the COVID-19 era, temperature and vaccination coverage played significant roles. The findings contribute valuable insights into the spatial and temporal patterns of SARS in Brazil, offering a foundation for targeted public health interventions and preparedness strategies.

## Files

### Dados_Regioes.csv

The data table "Dados_Regioes.csv" presents the number of SARS (Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome) cases grouped by epidemiological week and health region between 2016 and 2023.

### Temperatura.csv and Umidade.csv

The data tables "Temperatura.csv" and "Umidade.csv" present the weekly mean temperature and mean relative humidity (by epidemiological week) by health region between 2016 and 2023.

### Vacina_COVID_1_Dose.csv and Vacina_COVID_1_Dose_Per.csv

The data tables "Vacina_COVID_1_Dose.csv" and "Vacina_COVID_1_Dose_Per.csv" present the total number of 1st doses of the COVID-19 vaccine administered in Brazil and the 1st dose vaccination coverage rate between 2020 and 2023.

### Vacina_COVID_2_Dose.csv and Vacina_COVID_2_Dose_Per.csv

The data tables "Vacina_COVID_2_Dose.csv" and "Vacina_COVID_2_Dose_Per.csv" present the total number of 2nd doses of the COVID-19 vaccine administered in Brazil and the 2nd dose vaccination coverage rate between 2020 and 2023.

### Vacina_COVID_1_Ref.csv and Vacina_COVID_1_Reforco_Per.csv

The data tables "Vacina_COVID_1_Ref.csv" and "Vacina_COVID_1_Reforco_Per.csv" present the total number of 1st booster doses of the COVID-19 vaccine administered in Brazil and the 1st booster dose vaccination coverage rate between 2020 and 2023.

### Vacina_COVID_2_Ref.csv and Vacina_COVID_2_Reforco_Per.csv

The data tables "Vacina_COVID_2_Ref.csv" and "Vacina_COVID_2_Reforco_Per.csv" present the total number of 2nd booster doses of the COVID-19 vaccine administered in Brazil and the 2nd booster dose vaccination coverage rate between 2020 and 2023.
